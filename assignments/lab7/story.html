My transition from Window to MacOS

I've heard a lot about the benefits of MacOS, but when I first switched from Windows to Mac, I thought it was a really bad system.
Just like IOS system, too many transition animations. I click on something and need it to show up in front of me as quickly as
possible. And the Mac system does not support the installation of some software. Many scientific research software I needed at that
time required me to use a virtual machine to install and use it, which really annoyed me.

But because of the portability and long battery life of the MacBook, I insisted on using it. The other day I spent a night on youtube
learning how to be more productive with MacOS and it totally opened up a whole new world for me. The MacOS terminal and some task
scheduling are so efficient and orderly. I've stuck with MacOS ever since.
